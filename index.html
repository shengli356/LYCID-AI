The Pressing Needs

Construction projects can be chaotic and scary, and without real data, there aren't many options for managing the project effectively. Many construction companies turn to software like Microsoft Project, which can be a great tool for managing the schedule and budget of a project, but it requires a lot of customization and updating to truly work efficiently with your company's specific needs.

Why we are here

This is why we created Lucyd AI, an automated construction scheduling app that gathers real-time data from construction sites and provides automated construction schedules using artificial intelligence and machine learning. Lucyd AI uses ai technology to analyzes site progress and automatically generates weekly reports with recommended timelines and next steps for the construction developments.
